Your training starts today

This is a unique opportunity to work in the medical device industry without leaving your existing clinical role. Our training program enables you to develop industry skills and medical affairs capabilities while earning extra income and enhancing your career.

  1. Level 1 Foundation Training

    Take your first step into our Associates Program through our Foundation Training. You will learn essential principles of medical device regulation and applied literature reviewing, giving you immediate access to paid client work.

    • Detailed coverage of the EU MDR and medical device regulatory requirements
    • Application of existing skills to medical affairs roles
    • Practical exercises and guidance to apply knowledge to key tasks
    • Certificate of completion and immediate access to paid work with Mantra Systems

    £240 (inc. VAT)

    Register now

  2. Level 2 Lead Medical Writer

    Progress from project team member to team leader and become principal author on medical writing projects. Build upon experience from Level 1 and qualify for increased scope of work and enhanced payment packages.

    • Dedicated training in CER-writing, covering every section in specific detail
    • Detailed guidance on writing style, language selection and technical document review
    • Learn highly valuable skills and capabilities that can be applied across the medical device industry
    • Upon successful completion, work as lead author on medical writing projects at Mantra Systems

    Register now

  3. Level 3 Associates Mastery

    By invitation only, an opportunity for our most capable CER writers to apply their knowledge to advanced roles within the Mantra Systems Associates Program. Gain access to paid opportunities in extended roles including:

    • Review of CERs and technical documents written by others
    • Training of Medical Affairs Associates
    • Involvement in PMCF, Risk Management and extended roles within Mantra Systems
    • Opportunities in full-time employment and permanent career transition

    By invitation

Who is this for?

Our program is only open to fully licenced medical professionals. Proof of GMC registration (or equivalent if outside the UK) will be required.

What happens after I register?

After you have registered/paid for the Level 1: Foundation Training you will get access to our exclusive training platform, which contains all the videos and documents you need to complete this initial stage.

Is the course assessed?

Yes - a post-course exercise applies to each level of the program. Full support, guidance and personalised feedback will be provided.
