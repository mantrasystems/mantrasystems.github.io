We are medical device regulatory experts

Medical device regulation can be a complicated landscape to navigate. Ensure your medical devices are compliant by working with industry-leading specialists with a 100% regulatory submission success rate.

  • Services for you

    We offer targeted MDR & IVDR Consulting Services powered by the unique capabilities of our international team. We match your project to a subject matter expert with clinical experience in the field of your device, offering unparalleled clinical evidence generation, identification, appraisal & analysis.

    If you'd prefer to manage your regulatory processes in-house, our regulatory resources offers a range of digital products and services to support approval for your devices.

  • International

    Our clients are based around the world, vary in size from pre-market start-ups to stockmarket-listed multinationals and produce all classes of medical device across a wide range of clinical fields.
