Communicating Product Value – The ultimate guide to driving product adoption without competing on price

A well-constructed value proposition tailored to your audience can support higher prices through evidencing greater overall value. This reduces the risk of commoditisation and loss of market share to undifferentiated competitors.

But what is value? And how can value be effectively identified, framed and communicated to drive sales and build product adoption?

These questions are answered in our free eBook - a guide to defining and demonstrating value for your medical devices, informed by extensive cross-industry experience and delivered by a clinical expert.

Webinar: Using evidence to drive medical device sales

Co-hosted with Healthcare Economics & Outcomes Research expert Emanuela Castelnuovo

Using evidence in support of sales is now a basic necessity in any tender submission and payor engagement. But how can evidence be most effectively used in supporting sales and negotiating with buyers?

This webinar is for you if:

  • You are tasked with growing sales yet are facing increasing resistance from buyers
  • You believe that working with evidence will be too complicated, costly or time-consuming
  • You are looking for an edge over your competition that would enable you to hold price while increasing adoption
  • You would like to enter tenders and payor meetings with a dossier of targeted material that will appear to payors, professionals and patients alike

This unique webinar takes you step-by-step through processes and techniques to most effectively:

  • Overcome payor objections
  • Convey product value
  • Prepare for tenders & procurement submissions
  • Maximise the impact of marketing messaging and sales material
  • Tailor value propositions to the needs of multiple stakeholders, ensuring product adoption across the procurement chain
