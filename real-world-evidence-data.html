Real World Evidence (RWE) & Real World Data (RWD) services

Our specialised Real World Evidence & Real World Data systems, and suite of associated services will ensure EU MDR compliance of your medical devices within your existing resources.

RWE and higher quality clinical evidence are now required to CE-Mark medical devices. Obtaining RWE is difficult and resource-intensive.

We will work with you to design and develop your RWE generation platform and build it to the highest level of legal and regulatory compliance.

Our systems allow multiple locations to harmonise data entry, in a secure, user friendly experience, whilst adhering to ISO and GDPR standards.

Our RWE Generation Systems and MDR Compliance Services

  1. RWE Generation Systems

    We provide RWE generation systems allowing you to build your clinical evidence portfolio and deliver the Real World Evidence that you need.

  2. Clinical Evaluation Reports

    CERs are an essential component of medical device regulatory submissions. Changes imposed by MDR mean it is more challenging than ever before to meet obligations.

  3. Medical Risk Management

    The publication of the new risk management standard BS EN ISO 14971:2019 imposes more stringent requirements on manufacturers in performing structured risk analysis.

  4. Clinical Evidence Gap Analysis

    It can be difficult to determine exactly where deficiencies may lie in a clinical evidence portfolio. Do not invest in expensive research before determining where it is needed.

  5. Medical Complaints Handling

    The effective processing of medical complaints is a central component of effective medical device Quality Management. If you do not have in-house medical support, we can help.

  6. Post-Market Surveillance conduct

    MDR imposes strict requirements on manufacturers to develop a robust post-market surveillance system for their products. Benefit from our expertise when developing your PMS system.

  7. Clinical Competitor Analysis

    A properly structured analysis of a competitor's clinical evidence portfolio can reveal opportunities for significant competitive advantage.

  8. Clinical Evidence Application Training

    Empowering sales teams to effectively use your clinical data to drive product sales ensures a fantastic return on your research investment.
