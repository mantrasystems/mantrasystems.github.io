In more detail

Apply a simple and effective EU MDR Gap Analysis technique suitable for all medical device manufacturers.

  1. It is designed for any member of a medical deivce company with responsibility for ensuring alignment with EU MDR requirements. It does not require specialist regulatory knowledge.

    3. PMCF (Post-Market Clinical Follow-up)
    4. Clinical Evaluation
    5. Risk Management
    6. Other regulatory processes
