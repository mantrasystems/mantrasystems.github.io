Build your MDR compliance strategy

  • Post-Market Surveillance

    We will design, implement, and maintain your MDR compliant Post Market Surveillance (PMS) systems from scratch or update your MDD system for the new regulations.

  • Post-Market Clinical Follow-up

    We will develop your Post-Market Clinical Follow-up (PMCF) strategy to collect clinical data from your medical devices in order to produce real world evidence.

  • Clinical Evaluation

    Our full Clinical Evaluation solution involves a 360-degree analysis of your medical devices, writing and reviewing CERs and designing clinical development plans.

  • Gap Analysis

    Our medics will apply General Safety and Performance Requirements to your medical devices, to determine if any gaps in your current evidence portfolio.
